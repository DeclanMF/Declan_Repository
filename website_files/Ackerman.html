French Village International College® has been at the top of her game since she was established in January, 2006. The best secondary education institute in Badagry is well-known for her almost 75% admission rate and impressive academic prowess. Beside all this achievements, the school has struggled with technological advancements and generous funding despite the heavily demanding fees, but students have taken it upon their humble self to develop an official website for the school where admission information, up-to-date information about the school and even results are presumed to be displayed privately in the website.
